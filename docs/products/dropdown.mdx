# Dropdowns

Dropdowns are toggleable, contextual overlays for displaying lists of links

    <div className="d-flex align-items-center dropdown"> 
        <button className="btn dropdown-btn" role="button">Dropdown Button<i className="mi mi-chevron-down"></i></button>
        <ul className="dropdown-menu">
            <li className="dropdown-item active" role="button">Option 1</li>
            <li className="dropdown-item" role="button">Option 2</li>
            <li className="dropdown-item" role="button">Option 3</li>
        </ul>
    </div>
</div>

## Menu alignment - Right

By default, a dropdown menu is automatically positioned 100% from the top and along the left side
of its parent. Add .dropdown-menu-right to a .dropdown-menu to right align the dropdown menu.

<div className="de-code">
    <div className="btn-group dropdown"> 
        <button className="btn dropdown-btn" role="button" data-toggle="dropdown">Dropdown Button<i className="mi mi-chevron-down"></i></button>
        <div className="dropdown-menu dropdown-menu-right">
            <button className="dropdown-item" role="button">Option 1</button>
            <button className="dropdown-item" role="button">Option 2</button>
            <button className="dropdown-item disabled" role="button">Option 3</button>
            <hr/>
            <div className="button-group">
                <button className="btn btn-primary btn-sm">Add Project</button>
                <button className="btn btn-outline-primary btn-sm">Manage Projects</button>
            </div>
        </div>
    </div>
</div>
